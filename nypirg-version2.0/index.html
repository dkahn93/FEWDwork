We Work in the Public Interest

The New York Public Interest Research Group Fund (NYPIRG) is a non-partisan, nonprofit, research and public education organization. NYPIRG was founded in 1976 as an independent 501(c)(3) public outreach and education sister organization to the New York Public Interest Research Group.

Over the decades, we have educated hundreds of thousands of New Yorkers and recruited tens of thousands of community-based members from all parts of the state, enhancing NYPIRG’s institutional impact and helping to bring the voices of average New Yorkers to public policy debates.

NYPIRG’s full-time staff examines important issues, produces studies, and engages New Yorkers in public education campaigns designed to produce policies that strengthen democracy, enhance the rights of consumers and voters, and protect the environment and public health.

Fight Against Fossil Fuel Infrustructure

Right now we're working on climate change. We're seeing catastrophic weather conditions across the country (California, Texas, Florida, Puerto Rico, New York, etc.) and across the world. In order to mitigate the worst effects, we need to keep fossil fuels in the ground. So what are we doing?

We are fighting to pass a moritorium on all new types of fossil fuel infustructure. We have an excess of energy in the state. We do not need more dirty fracked gas electric power plants, storage facilities, and pipelines locking us into decades more of the status quo. Our food, water, and health are at risk.

NYPIRG Youth Civic Engagement Fellowship

Are you passionate about social justice, economic equality, and environmental protection? Do you want to address the problems that you see affecting your family and community? If so, apply today to the NYPIRG Youth Civic Engagement Fellowship for the fall 2018 academic semester!

For decades, NYPIRG has trained young organizers on how to build political momentum and become leading voices in their communities. Activists working with NYPIRG have gone on to be journalists, attorneys, campaign strategists, union organizers, and even a former President of the United States. NYPIRG’s civic engagement model is a hands-on, experiential dive into the world of advocacy. Turn your passion for social change into real policy by engaging your community and leading them to a better future. Apply by August 3, 2018.

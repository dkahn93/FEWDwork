About

Founded in the Summer of 2018, Spot the Green will find near you a treasure of healthy and locally sourced and deliciously food, ready-to-bottle water, and more. If you're looking for a way to change up your lifestyle, keep this website handy. More features coming soon.

Side Note:
If you're looking for a way to get involved, I'm looking for you. The public drinking fountains that I have mapped out in the city are good and all, but what if you're not near one? What if they're turned off? That's why I'm looking to build a database on all shops that offer free water (without having to ask for it), beit by water tank or through a small spout by the counter (I would expect most of these locations to be in coffee shops, but not all). When you find a location, please click the contact button on the nav and send your info.
